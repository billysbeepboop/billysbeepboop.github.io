About Me

My professional experiences and preferences lean towards working with mixed-domain mechatronic systems that leverage the hard, soft, and firm to do radically novel things. I particularly excel in wide-open problem spaces within robotics and automation, and cherish all that comes with having high ownership, creative agency, and simultaneous problems to solve. What my career so far has made clear, though, is that any problem I can get my teeth into, regardless of context, will reward me. In all cases, I try to be proactive in pursuit of collaborators, feedback, and consensus to make the smartest engineering decisions in the face of conflicting time, resource, and scope pressures.

Original and tangible outcomes are the common thread in everything I enjoy - all of my many hobbies involve making one thing or other, and it's pretty much always been this way for me; the unexpected challenges and learning opportunities never get old.
